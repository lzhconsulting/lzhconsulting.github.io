What We Do

We provide cost effective, strategic occupational health and ergonomic consulting services for business, office, industry and healthcare.

Our core sectors of expertise are in worksite analysis, hazard prevention and control, medical management, training and education, occupational health and ergonomic programs, return to work, physical demands and process improvement.

With LZH Consulting, you can provide a safer working environment for your employees and lower your workers' compensation and medical costs.
